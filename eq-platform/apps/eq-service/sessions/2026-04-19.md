## What shipped

### 1 — Demo account unlocked for report generation

- `UPDATE tenant_members SET role='supervisor'` for `demo@eqsolves.com.au` (user `b0000000-…-01`) in the Demo Electrical tenant (`a0000000-…-01`). Was `technician` — now `supervisor`.
- Why: `/api/acb-report` and `/api/compliance-report` gate on `canWrite(role)`, which excludes `technician` and `read_only`. `issueMaintenanceReportAction` already allows technicians but the raw dashboard export endpoints do not. Promoting the single demo user is the smallest possible change — no code edits, no semantic shift for other tenants.
- Impact: anyone logged into the demo can now click **Generate Report** on `/reports` and download a real compliance DOCX, and can hit the ACB report endpoint from the per-site ACB view.

### 2 — Seeded ACB readings at Harborview Data Centre

- The 3 completed Harborview ACB tests (`20000000-…-01` Pass, `-02` Pass, `-03` Defect) had **zero** readings. Seeded 51 readings per test covering:
  - Visual/Functional quick items (3)
  - Visual/Functional checklist (27 items across Visual Inspection, Mechanical parts degreasing/greasing, Device Functional Check, Auxiliaries Check, Device Racking In)
  - Electrical: Contact Resistance R/W/B (µΩ), IR Closed (9 combos, MΩ), IR Open (4 combos, MΩ), Secondary Injection, Operation Counter before/after, Protection rows (Short/Inst/Long)
  - On the Defect test: Red-phase contact resistance seeded at 71µΩ vs ~44µΩ on others — triggers the generator's 30% variance flag. Main-contact-wear row seeded as `isPass=false` with remedial notes.
- Why: so both the live demo report and our website samples show a complete, believable output — not a mostly-empty shell.
