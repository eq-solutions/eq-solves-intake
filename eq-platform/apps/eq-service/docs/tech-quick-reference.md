# EQ Service — Tech Quick Reference

> One page. Print double-sided if you want notes on the back. Keep it in your kit.

---

## Start of day

1. Open **eq-solves-service.netlify.app** on your phone (bookmark it for one-tap).
2. Sign in with your work email and password.
3. If prompted, enter your 6-digit MFA code from your authenticator app.
4. You land on **Do** — the action hub. Or tap **Dashboard** for the daily overview.
5. From the sidebar, tap **Maintenance** to see today's checks.
6. The list defaults to **Mine** — your assigned checks only. Tap **All** to see the whole team's work if you need to.

---

## Run a check (on site)

1. Tap the check name to open it.
2. Tap **Start Check** at the top (or in the sticky bar at the bottom of the screen on mobile).
3. For each task: tap the green check ✅ for **Pass**, the red X ❌ for **Fail**, or the dash ➖ for **N/A**.
4. To add a note for a task, tap the notes field next to the buttons. Type your note. Tap outside to save.
5. If every task is the same result, you can tap **Complete All Assets** at the top to bulk-pass — *only on PPM checks*, not ACB/NSX/RCD tests.
6. When everything's done, tap **Complete Check**. If a required task is unfinished, the button will say "*N required tasks left*" instead.

**Stuck?** A required task you can't complete on site? Add a note explaining why, mark it N/A, and raise a defect for the issue.

---

## Add a photo

1. On the check page, scroll to **Attachments**.
2. Tap **Upload**.
3. Your phone camera opens directly. Take the photo.
4. The photo uploads and shows as a thumbnail in the list.
5. Tap the thumbnail any time to view the full image.

You can also add a photo when raising a defect — same flow, same camera button.

---

## Raise a defect

1. From inside a check, find the **Defects** section.
2. Tap **+ Raise Defect**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — short, e.g. "ACB-3 trip unit faulty"
   - **Severity** — low / medium / high / critical
   - **Description** — what you saw, what you did
4. Add a photo via **Upload** (camera opens automatically).
5. Tap **Save**.

The defect goes to the defect register and your supervisor is notified.

## When something goes wrong

- **App is slow or won't load** — close and reopen the browser. If still bad, try mobile data instead of site Wi-Fi (basement plant rooms often have poor signal).
- **Can't sign in** — wrong password three times locks you out for a few minutes. Wait, then try again. If still stuck, message Royce.
- **MFA code not accepted** — your phone clock might be off. Open your authenticator app, force-refresh. If still stuck, use a recovery code.
- **Photo won't upload** — check your signal. The app holds your photo locally; once signal returns, retry the upload.
- **Wrong tenant / can't see your work** — sign out, sign in again. If still wrong, message Royce.

## Done at end of day

1. Make sure every check you started today is **Complete** or has a clear note explaining where you got up to.
2. Make sure every defect has a photo and a description.
3. Sign out from the sidebar (so the next person on the device doesn't see your work).
